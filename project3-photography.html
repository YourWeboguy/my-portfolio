Signals that triggered the project

  • ▹ Inbox chaos: three tools, no single queue, and SLA breaches climbing.
  • ▹ Engineers pulled into triage because key account context was missing.
  • ▹ Leadership lacked real-time insight into support load and themes.

Automation highlights

  • ▹ AI tagging on Intercom routes tickets by intent, severity, and account tier.
  • ▹ Notion knowledge base articles suggested instantly inside the Intercom inbox.
  • ▹ Priority issues create Linear bugs with screenshots and Loom recordings.
  • ▹ Slack alerts summarize daily volume, SLA risk, and customers needing follow-up.

The workflow

01 · Intake

Intercom webhooks pass payloads into AWS Lambda. Messages receive language detection, sentiment scoring, and SLA tagging.

02 · Context

Customer metadata is enriched from Notion, Stripe, and Productboard to give agents a 360° view inside Intercom.

03 · Action

Linear tickets spin up automatically with checklists, owners, and acceptance criteria pre-filled.

04 · Insight

Metrics and themes roll into Metabase dashboards and a weekly Loom recap for leadership.

Tooling + safeguards

  • ▹ Make scenarios orchestrate handoffs with path splitting for VIP accounts.
  • ▹ AWS Lambda functions manage message parsing, translation, and Slack formatting.
  • ▹ Rollbar logging captures every payload and retry for compliance reviews.
  • ▹ Notion HQ houses SOPs, escalation trees, and real-time status dashboards.

Team enablement

  • ▹ 9 Loom tutorials showing agents how to handle exceptions.
  • ▹ Slack Q&A channel monitored by Jordan for the first 30 days post-launch.
  • ▹ KPI scorecards embedded in Notion for daily standups.
