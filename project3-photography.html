The challenge

Tickets were routed manually, SLAs slipped on weekends, and leadership had no insight into issue themes. The team wanted guardrails that kept humans in the loop while removing repetitive triage work.

The mission: reduce first-response times, capture richer data on every conversation, and keep the team confident about AI assistance.

Success snapshot

  • • 58% faster first response and 39% faster resolution
  • • 72% of inbound tickets handled with AI-suggested macros
  • • CSAT lifted from 4.2 to 4.8 within the first quarter

Automation pillars

Smart routing matrix

Airtable houses pod availability and expertise. Make flows assign tickets based on load, priority, and language.

AI macro assistant

OpenAI analyzes context, suggests macros, and drafts human-ready replies. Agents approve with one click.

Health score dashboards

Airtable + Retool dashboards highlight volume spikes, backlog risk, and accounts requiring executive follow-up.

Knowledge loop

Closed tickets with high effort automatically trigger a Scribe capture and Slab documentation request.

Operational rhythm

  • Week 1: Service blueprinting, ticket taxonomy design, and AI risk review.
  • Week 2: Routing automations, data migrations, and QA safety checks.
  • Week 3: Macro assistant rollout with shadow mode and human approval loop.
  • Weeks 4-5: Dashboard launch, training cohorts, and leadership reporting cadences.

Outcomes

Agents now start each shift with a live queue prioritized by risk. Leadership sees emerging themes and high-effort accounts in a single view, while AI suggestions keep quality consistent without replacing judgment.

The support org now runs a bi-weekly optimization backlog reviewing automations, macros, and documentation to ensure the system keeps evolving with customer needs.
