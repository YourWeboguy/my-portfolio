Client onboarding automation

FlowLaunch: Intake to delivery in 21 days

Built for a collective of fractional COOs that needed to standardize discovery, kickoff, and delivery. We replaced spreadsheets and ad-hoc emails with a governed automation suite that keeps every pod in sync.

Duration
4 weeks
Team served
18 operators
Systems
Typeform · HubSpot · ClickUp · Slack

The challenge

Each onboarding had 40+ manual touchpoints. Notes lived in Airtable, tasks in Asana, and client updates in scattered Slack channels. With six pods handling overlapping clients, the leadership team lacked visibility into status, capacity, and blockers.

The goal: unify intake, qualification, task generation, and communications while preserving the personalized touch that clients expected from a premium operations partner.

Success snapshot

  • • 63% faster kickoff from signed agreement to active workspace
  • • 42 manual steps reduced to 9 automated checkpoints
  • • Health dashboard adopted by every pod lead within two weeks

Architecture highlights

Structured intake & scoring

Typeform responses sync to HubSpot with property validation, dedupe checks, and an automated scoring model that flags high-complexity accounts for senior review.

Auto-generated task packs

Make scenarios orchestrate ClickUp templates, due dates, and dependencies based on services purchased, ensuring each pod follows the same playbook.

Slack copilots

A shared Slack channel posts milestone updates, escalations, and approval requests with buttons to reassign or snooze tasks without leaving the thread.

Observability & QA

Error handling logs in a Notion control tower, while nightly QA runs verify data syncs and notify the stewardship team when anything drifts.

Timeline & ceremonies

  • Week 1: Discovery interviews, workflow mapping, success metrics alignment.
  • Week 2: Architecture design, sandbox builds, and approval checkpoints.
  • Week 3: Automation development, QA with real data, and fail-safe scripting.
  • Week 4: Enablement, handover docs, and first 30-day optimization backlog.

Results that stuck

Pods now have a shared view of every onboarding, with alerts that surface when deliverables lag or approvals are overdue. Leadership receives a Monday digest summarizing launch velocity, blockers, and forecasted capacity needs.

The automation suite freed 20+ operator hours per week, reduced onboarding churn to zero for three consecutive quarters, and gave the client confidence to launch a productized service tier.
