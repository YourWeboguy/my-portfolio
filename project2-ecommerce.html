RevOps CRM transformation

RevBeam: A single source of revenue truth

A hybrid outbound/inbound sales team juggled five disconnected tools with no shared metrics. We consolidated everything into HubSpot, automated commissions, and surfaced a leadership dashboard that finally matched finance’s numbers.

Duration
6 weeks
Team served
22 sellers
Systems
HubSpot · Aircall · Chargebee · Notion

The challenge

SDRs worked out of spreadsheets, AEs ran their own Airtable boards, and RevOps stitched numbers together in Looker every Friday. Commission disputes were common and churn signals went unnoticed until renewals were at risk.

We set out to create a single workspace that connected pipeline, product usage, billing, and renewal health so leadership could coach proactively.

Success snapshot

  • • Four data sources unified with near real-time syncs
  • • 4× improvement in forecasting accuracy
  • • Commission reconciliation completed in 90 minutes vs. two days

What we built

Lifecycle blueprint

Redesigned HubSpot pipelines with clear lifecycle stages, required fields, and stage exit criteria to clean up reporting.

Usage-driven alerts

Chargebee MRR movements and product usage events stream into HubSpot custom objects, driving churn and expansion playbooks.

Commission automation

A Notion control center calculates payouts by role, applies accelerators, and syncs approvals to finance in Slack.

Leadership dashboards

Net-new, expansion, and churn forecasts rendered in HubSpot + Looker Studio with drill-down by pod, industry, and ACV.

Timeline & enablement

  • Weeks 1-2: Process audit, data cleanup, and architecture sign-off.
  • Week 3: Pipeline migration, integration wiring, and QA scenarios.
  • Week 4: Dashboard design with RevOps + leadership workshops.
  • Weeks 5-6: Commission automation, training library, and go-live shadowing.

Outcomes

Sellers now work from a single HubSpot view with contextual product data. Weekly forecast meetings focus on coaching, not reconciling numbers. Finance receives approved commissions automatically, and leadership trusts the dashboard to plan hiring.

The client now reviews pipeline health every Monday, churn risk alerts fire within 30 minutes of a usage dip, and the RevOps team reclaimed ten hours per week previously spent on manual reporting.
