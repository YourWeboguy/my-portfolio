System architecture

Data layer

Airtable bases split into Revenue, Finance, and Partners with synced tables and granular permissions.

  • • 12 synced tables
  • • 5 interface dashboards
  • • 63 automations

Integration layer

Stripe, QuickBooks, and HubSpot connect via Make scenarios with webhook retries and change logs.

  • • 8 Make scenarios
  • • Error handling + Slack alerts
  • • Audit logs stored in Supabase

Experience layer

Softr portal with role-based access for partners, leadership scorecards in Metabase, and Notion SOP hub.

  • • 4 partner tiers
  • • Auto-generated renewal briefings
  • • Weekly Loom walkthroughs

Dashboards operators love

Interactive interfaces provide drill-downs into pipeline health, expansion revenue, and partner performance. Leadership receives scheduled PDFs while reps work in Airtable Interface Designer.
