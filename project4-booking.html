Partner insights portal

PartnerPulse: Executive dashboards on demand

A venture studio needed a central hub to manage 40+ channel partners, record meetings, and share quarterly plans. We built a Notion + Supabase workspace with automated call notes and scorecards that gave leadership the clarity to scale.

Duration
5 weeks
Team served
Leadership + 12 partner managers
Systems
Notion · Supabase · Fathom · Slack

The challenge

Partner conversations were captured in Google Docs, KPIs lived in Sheets, and leadership reviews took days to compile. Teams needed a single truth source for partner health and next actions.

The mandate: centralize data, automate note-taking, and build dashboards executives trust before stepping into QBRs.

Success snapshot

  • • 12 hours of reporting prep saved every week
  • • 94% active adoption from partner managers
  • • Quarterly plans auto-generated with live performance data

Portal foundations

Unified partner records

Supabase acts as the single database powering Notion dashboards with revenue, pipeline, and satisfaction metrics.

AI meeting summaries

Fathom recordings sync into Notion with auto-tagged highlights and action items routed to responsible owners.

Scorecards & alerts

Weekly Slack digests flag partners slipping on KPIs, while dashboards visualize trends by region and solution area.

Quarterly planning kit

Templates generate QBR agendas, mutual action plans, and resource requests without manual copy/paste.

Launch plan

  • Week 1: Discovery, data audit, and partner segmentation workshops.
  • Week 2: Supabase schema build, Notion workspace wireframes, and integration proofs.
  • Week 3: Dashboard development, AI note capture pilots, and feedback loops.
  • Weeks 4-5: Enablement, documentation, and adoption scorecards.

Outcomes

Partner managers now run their week from one control center. Leadership reviews revenue, pipeline, and partner sentiment in minutes, not hours. Automated call notes keep strategy top of mind and ensure action items land with the right owner.

The studio has since doubled its partner portfolio without adding operations headcount, relying on the portal’s alerts and playbooks to maintain quality.
